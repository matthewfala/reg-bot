Office of Academic Records and Registrar

 

Audited Courses:
  • Course work taken on an audit basis will be assessed at the current tuition rate.
  • Course work taken on an audit basis will be assessed at the current tuition rate.

  • A course taken for audit will not receive credit and will not appear on the USC transcript or grade report.
  • A course taken for audit is not included in enrollment for purposes of receiving financial aid.
  • Under no circumstances will the university allow a petition to request to change the registration status of a course from a letter grade or credit to audit (V) or vice versa after the 20% deadline of the session in which a course is scheduled.
Pass/No Pass Graded Work

For Undergraduate Students:

  • WRIT-150 and WRIT-340 may not be taken on a P/NP basis.
  • Courses numbered 490 may not be taken on a P/NP basis.
  • A maximum of 4 P/NP units may be applied to the General Education (GE) requirement.
  • A maximum of 24 units taken on a P/NP basis may be used toward an undergraduate degree.
  • Course work required for a minor may not be taken on a P/NP basis.
  • The use of P/NP course work to fulfill major requirements must be approved in writing by the academic department.
  • Individual departments may have further restrictions on whether courses taken for P/NP may be used to fulfill specific requirements.
  • All students should consult with their academic advisor before enrolling for any course on a P/NP basis.

For Graduate Students:

  • Course work taken on a P/NP basis cannot be applied toward a graduate degree.
  • Individual departments may have further restrictions on whether courses taken for P/NP may be used to fulfill specific requirements.
  • All students should consult with their academic advisor before enrolling for any course on a P/NP basis.
